**MXToolbox Email Investigation**

**Objective:**

- Analyze public DNS and MX records to understand email routing infrastructure.

**Tools Used:**

- MXToolbox
  
**Check Performed**  

- MX Record Lookup
- DNS Lookup on MX servers
- SPF Record Lookup
- DKIM Record Lookup
- DMARC Record Lookup

**Steps Performed:**

- Performed MX Lookup on gmail.com.
Identified Google's mail exchange servers.
- Conducted DNS lookup on the discovered mail servers.
- Reviewed SPF records to identify authorized email senders.
- Examined DKIM records used for email authentication.
- Analyzed DMARC policies for email spoofing protection.
Documented observations and findings

**Findings:**

- Identified Google's mail servers and associated DNS records.
- Observed how MX records support email delivery and routing.
- Verified SPF, DKIM, and DMARC email security configurations.
- Gained understanding of email routing and authentication mechanisms.
